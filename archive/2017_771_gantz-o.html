Gantz: O

Just a quick review of the new Gantz: O movie, currently on Netflix. I remember seeing the anime when it first came out, back in 2004, but I didn't really enjoy it. This new movie is somewhat of an improvement. Better paced and more enjoyable to watch, probably due to the action being squeezed into a single movie, versus drawing it out for an entire season. Looking around, I saw they also made a live action film back in 2011. I haven't seen that one yet, so I can't really comment.
